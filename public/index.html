Air Traffic Control Simulator

3D Air Traffic Control Simulator done with C++ and OpenGl. Aircraft would appear randomly at different times and places near the airport an the algorithm should design an efficient path for each of them so they can land and navigate evading collisions in the least amount of time. Each path was heavyly resticted to follow also the navigation rules that real aircraft are subjected(minimum distance bitween aircraft, maximum and minimum speed at landing, maximum acceleration and deceleration,...). The algorithm created and finally implemented could coordinate the trajectories of more than 30 aircrafts without any collision.

  • C++ and OpenGL
  • OOP
  • Trajectory Planning
